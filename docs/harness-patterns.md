# Harness Patterns

Harness patterns are reusable work shapes that help humans and AI collaborate consistently.

## Meeting / thread harness

Use when a conversation is evolving across meetings, Teams threads, email, or issue comments.

Capture:

- Name of the thread
- Participants
- Background
- Current status
- Decisions made
- Assumptions
- Open questions
- Active owner
- Next action

AI role:

- Maintain continuity
- Identify contradictions
- Draft concise updates
- Separate facts from interpretation

Human role:

- Decide what is true
- Decide what should be shared
- Keep accountable owners in control

## Investigation harness

Use when diagnosing technical or operational ambiguity.

Capture:

- Hypothesis
- Evidence
- Query / tool used
- Result
- Interpretation
- Alternative explanations
- Next validation step

AI role:

- Challenge overconfident conclusions
- Suggest distinguishing tests
- Track what changed

Human role:

- Run approved tools
- Validate with domain experts
- Own final conclusion

## Decision harness

Use when choosing between options.

Capture:

- Decision needed
- Options
- Pros / cons
- Risks
- Reversibility
- Required approvers
- Decision record

AI role:

- Structure tradeoffs
- Surface missing stakeholders
- Draft decision notes

Human role:

- Make the decision
- Accept accountability

## Learning capture harness

Use after a meaningful event, incident, project, or correction.

Capture:

- What happened
- What surprised us
- What pattern emerged
- What should change
- Where the learning should live

AI role:

- Convert raw experience into reusable guidance
- Suggest evals or checklists

Human role:

- Approve what becomes durable knowledge
